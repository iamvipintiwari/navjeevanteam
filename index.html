About Us

Who we are ?

Navjeevan is a caring family of dedicated student volunteers working under the able guidance of the seasoned mentors, to lend a helping hand to provide educational assistance to the underprivileged children.

What we do ?

We work for the smiles of deprived children. Apart from encouraging and motivating them to learn, we also provide them with basic primary education. After building the foundation, we work towards linking them to the mainstream of education. We also conduct regular events and workshops, loaded with interactive activities with the children, to make their learning fun and effective.

Our Vision

We, the people at Navjeevan are wholly committed to work for : improving the quality of life of marginalized sections of society, providing quality education in government schools, bringing children who so far are deprived of education in the fold of mainstream education and for overall development of youth.
