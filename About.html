Aksharam

The objective Aksharam is to infuse the spirit of learning and providing quality education among children who are so far deprived of it.
We believe that every individual is entitled to a minimum basic level of education. Basic literacy is a fundamental human right and it also plays a pivotal role to motivate children to pursue formal education. Thus, Aksharam was started with an aim to engage children in a preliminary learning experience through educational and interactive activities at their doorstep (nearest centre) to make them eager and interested to learn further. We reach out the non-enrolled/dropout children in their locality and conduct regular evening classes for the children as a first step towards connecting them to mainstream education. Through its various centers, Aksharam has touched the lives of around 350 children, by providing them basic primary education.

Prerak

The objective of Prerak is to make efforts for improving the quality of life of marginalized sections of society, along with integrating mainstream education with children and collaborating with government schools to facilitate the services provided by administration to children.
Although learning can begin from anywhere, still the role of a regular school cannot be ruled out, as it provides the children an ideal environment for learning. From our past experiences we have realized the fact that many needy people are not able to grab the opportunities due to lack of awareness about the various schemes and programs. Prerak act as a link between the children aspiring for mainstream formal education (drop outs and Non-enrolled) and the institutions working for this cause. It creates awareness, counsels and motivates the children and their guardians about various opportunities for education suitable for them and undertakes frequent follow ups, assisting them through the admission process. 150+ children have been successfully connected to the mainstream of education through the efforts of Prerak wing.

Navkriti

The objective of Navkriti is to enhance the skill development of children and to make favourable conditions in government schools for management and inculcation of quality education.
We feel that one of the many purposes of effective learning is to motivate and enable young minds to express their creativity. Any task can be enhanced by doing it with a touch of innovative ideas. Thus, we have realized the role of a constructive mind-set that needs to be developed by both, the children and the volunteers, to take learning to an altogether different level. We intend to capture our energy into something constructive. The Navkriti Wing is a creative think tank of Navjeevan which works for enriching the creative sensibilities and possibilities for skill development of the children by conducting various engaging activities, events and workshops. Shaurya, the annual flagship event of Navjeevan, which has been organized successfully since last four years on 26th January.
