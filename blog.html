“Prerak- A step towards education”

The inception of the Prerak campaign was initiated in 2019, February. The main objective of this campaign is to facilitate admission of all the school dropouts in government schools of Indore, and to strive for Indore to make it best in educational standards by every means.
The campaign was divided into two phases:- First Phase : Public Awareness and Survey Second Phase : Admission Process

Prerak : First Phase

In the first phase, at these six locations, two teams were assigned. First for Public Awareness campaign and second team for surveying education deprived and dropouts of school. There have been thirty five active members in our team. To spread public awareness and to reach out to youth, our team reached out to all the universities in Indore for an open dialogue. We got thorough support of 200 youth in running this campaign. During this time we reached out to 35 slum areas and met around 2000 families, surveying and spreading our campaign. We found 920 school dropouts and non school going children during this survey.

Prerak : Second Phase

The survey of the Prerak Movement was fully online. Thus, after the survey, all the government schools in Indore were provided with the list of school dropouts and education deprived children who are not attending any schools. Hence, these children were admitted to schools. To make Prerak campaign a success, we got enthusiastic response and support from all the organizations and education departments in Indore.

“Shaurya - An Expression of Pride”

Every year, 80-100 children get an opportunity to participate in the program and Navjeevan volunteers groom them for arts and cultural performances. To show the zeal and pride for the country, Navjeevan started this unique initiative from January 26th, 2017. The objective of Shaurya is to provide a platform for children where they could literally live the emotion of national pride and showcase their talents through cultural and art performances.
